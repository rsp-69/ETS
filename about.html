ETS — Employer Tracking System

A personal operating system built to counterbalance the hiring world’s Applicant Tracking Systems. Where ATS filters applicants, ETS empowers you — helping you present your story with clarity, identity, and intention.

Levelling the Playing Field

Companies automate their side of the process, so you built a system to automate yours — but with humanity. ETS exists to give you control, confidence, and a fair chance to be seen for who you actually are.

Personalisation as Respect

You’ve always believed a résumé should feel like it was made for the company, not just sent to them. ETS turns that belief into a system — tailoring colours, tone, and content to match each organisation’s identity.

Brand Aware. Job Aware. Me Aware.

ETS reads job descriptions, matches them with your achievements, and generates company‑specific résumés and cover letters. It’s part résumé builder, part portfolio, part OS — designed to showcase your work and how you think.
